Grill & Flame


As one of the first restaurants on Chicago’s famed Restaurant Row in the West Loop,
Grill & Flame began in 2010 with a goal of serving bold, global flavors to our local
community (and visitors!). Since then, our desire to treat guests like family has remained
the same (as have our Green Beans) – but our seasonal flavors, cocktails, wine and
locally-rooted beer are ever-changing.

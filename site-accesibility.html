Website Accessibility Statement

Boka Restaurant Group is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone, and applying the relevant accessibility standards. Boka Restaurant Group has implemented, and continues to update its implementation, of the relevant portions of the World Wide Web Consortium’s Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 Level AA (WCAG 2.1 AA) as its web accessibility standard or other standards to the extent required by the Americans with Disabilities Act (“ADA”)

Contact Boka Restaurant Group with your accessibility questions

Please be aware that our efforts are ongoing. If, at any time, you have specific questions or concerns about the accessibility of any particular web page on any of our restaurant sites, please contact our assigned Website Accessibility Officer using the contact information below:
